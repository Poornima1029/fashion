Fashion Trends

Traditional Saree

A timeless classic, the saree is an epitome of grace. Whether it's silk, cotton, or chiffon, this attire represents elegance and Indian heritage.

Lehenga Choli

A traditional yet trendy ensemble, the lehenga choli is the go-to outfit for weddings and festive occasions, often featuring intricate embroidery and vibrant colors.

Kurta with Palazzo

The fusion of traditional kurtas with palazzo pants offers comfort and style, perfect for casual wear as well as semi-formal events.

Bandhani Prints

Originating from Rajasthan and Gujarat, Bandhani tie-dye prints are vibrant and full of character, widely used in sarees, dupattas, and lehengas.

Angrakha Style

A crossover style that ties on the side, the Angrakha is inspired by royal attire and is often worn as kurtas or dresses, showcasing fine craftsmanship and luxury.
